The purpose of the Gradient Blog is to locate us online and within our earnestness. An exit from the demoralizing and self-serious ironies of online fora. A Gradient Blog looks vaguely silly; it's probably not the place for your bombshell theses and arguments :-) And there is no one here, so it's impossible to make a proper intervention into any debate from this platform (which is not a platform).

Some of the ☼thinking☼ behind this project comes from the following:
Mervyn Peake writing that "...In a dream world, all is logic." Freud paraphrasing Aristotle in the Introductory Lectures: "The dream is the life of the mind during sleep, a life bearing certain resemblances to our waking life and, at the same time, differing from it widely." Those kinds of definitions, which come up in 'On Denoting:' "...we must abandon the view that the denotation is what is concerned in propositions which contain denoting phrases." In the dream world, there is no difference between "Koriskos" and Koriskos!

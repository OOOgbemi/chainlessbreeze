Answer* these SIX questions and you will finally be a poet...but remember! The idea is a poetry practice for everyone guided by self-pity à la Highsmith. We can all do it. The opposite of poetry in some ways is bravado. It's okay to be angry. In fact, it will help you to be a poet. You can be angry here. You're not going to hurt anyone.

❆Which Boats Have Sailed?


❆Which Sin, Mischief, or Serious Wrongdoing Are You Not Sorry About? (can be multiple)


❆Whose Life Do You Most Wish You Had, And Why?


❆Tell Me Your Boring Dream (can be multiple)


❆When Last Were You A Liar?


❆Having Answered ALL Of The Questions Above, What Would You Say Is The Color Of Your Poem?

Nice work, you're finally a poet! Shut down your computer, take out your pen and notebook, and wallow a little. It's a luxury, but one that's yours, and whatever you write now is bound to mean something_













*I have no way of collecting your answers and no wish to. However, it is very important in the context for this exercise and other reasons that you feel secure enough to be honest with yourself. Instead of typing your answers here, feel free to use a notebook or whatever feels most private. ☺

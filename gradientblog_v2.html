What is a Gradient Blog ?

A Gradient Blog is a very simple, one-page blog template. It can be customized, but in its standard form it is designed to hold a single ~125 word entry or post, titled 'Today,' and one image.

The feeling/idea behind this blog design was earnestness. I've heard and read people who are around my age (26 in 2025) express nostalgia for more earnest ways of being on the web, and I relate to that. For those of us who intend to stay online in coming years, it may matter to have access to templates like these and to create platforms that are not 'platforms.'

A Gradient Blog can hold your 'status,' a poem, an essay you're drafting, an oblique message... It's yours!
